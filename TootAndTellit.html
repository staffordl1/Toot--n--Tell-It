About Us

We, Toot 'N' Tell It, are a family owned restaurant here in the town of Dyer, Tennessee.

The Toot 'N' Tell It that you can see today has been in its very spot since the year 1993.

History

In the 1950s, before we moved to Dyer, Toot 'N' Tell It was placed in Martin as a frozen custard building. However, it was soon bought by two men named Harold Brouder and Wallace Estest. Afterwards, Toot 'N' Tell It was owned by Buck Prater, Dot Prater, and Joe Campbell. These would be the owners of Toot 'N' Tell It all up until it was relocated to Dyer, in 1993, as a car hop restaurant. Shortly after, Toot 'N' Tell It was owned by owned by Carol Zimmerman, Deavan Zimmerman, Ginger Zimmerman, and "Granny", who it has been owned by up until today.

Fun fact: both the Old Fasion Cheeseburger and Burger menu items developed from the original Toot 'N' Tell It.
